Autobiography

Hi! I am Yi-Lin Chiang, or you can just call my English name, Elynn. I’m currently a sophomore in NTHU, Taiwan, majoring in FLL and growing interest in learning German language. Hsinchu City is my hometown, and to be exactly, Nanliao is the place where I’ve been lived for years.

During my leisure time, I like to do various kinds of sports. There’s an advantage that NTHU has plenty of resources. I can randomly go for a jog in the sport field, join volleyball practices with teammates at night, or enjoy playing table tennis on weekends. Under a friend’s encouragement, I’ve also started to experience bouldering since the fresh year and found it intriguing. I believe that regularly exercising can lead to positive effect, and sometimes, even a few unexpected friendships. Overall, participating in these sports not only strengthen my body but also help invigorate my spirit.

Before the busy high school days, reading fictions used to be one of my favorite pastimes. I liked to borrow well-received series like Harry Potter, every work from Jin Yong, or sometimes the translated version of foreign classics, such as Jane Austen’s works. Bringing back those memories, I always appreciate that these literary works have brought about imagination and enriched my early adolescence. Needless to say, they show readers all over the world a significant power of words. In the end, they also fuel my passion in pursuing languages and literature in college.

歡迎來到我的網站!我是江宜霖，來自新竹南寮，目前為一名就讀清大外文系的大二生，並且對於學習德語感到興趣。閒暇時除了瀏覽一些影集或是YouTube上的影片之外，我也喜歡聆聽音樂和嘗試各式各樣的運動，如慢跑、打排球、桌球、或是和朋友一起到攀岩場體驗等等。對我來說彈鋼琴是最好親近古典音樂的方式，也是長久以來一直陪伴著我的興趣。這些不同的嗜好和文學一樣，皆有舒緩人心的效果，在我偶爾陷入疲累的時候能夠幫助轉換心情、釋放情緒。

高中以前，我喜歡徜徉在小說的世界之中，並閱讀了每一本金庸的作品、暢銷的哈利波特小說，與一些外國經典的翻譯作品，例如《飄》、《基督山恩仇記》和《傲慢與偏見》等等，即使因時間過去許久對於其中的內容情節不復以往的熟悉，但是回想起那些時光，我總是很感激有文學的陪伴，開闊了我的視野與豐富我的想像力，最後更將我成功地推向現在研究外國語文學的這條路上。
